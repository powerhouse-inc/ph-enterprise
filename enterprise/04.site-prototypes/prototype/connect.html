What Connect Does

Where your teams do the work.

Structured Business Data

Every business object follows a typed schema — invoices, contracts, expense reports, scope-of-work documents. Schemas enforce consistency: the data is always valid by construction, not by manual validation.

Drive Apps & Workflow Views

Drive apps overlay the file system with specialized workflow interfaces — standardized best-practice processes presented directly, rather than leaving operators to organize their own folder hierarchies.

Real-Time Collaboration

Documents synchronize across users and devices through their operation histories. Multiple team members can work on the same business objects simultaneously. Changes are attributed, versioned, and mergeable.

Human Oversight of AI

AI agents operating through Clint act on the same business data visible in Connect. Operators see what agents have done, can review agent-generated changes, approve or reject operations, and maintain oversight.

Offline-Capable

Local-first architecture means Connect works without a network connection. Operations are queued locally and synchronized when connectivity returns. No data loss, no interrupted workflows.

Cryptographic Verification

Every operation is signed by its author — human or agent. The full history is offline-verifiable without trusting a central authority. Tamper-proof audit trail as an inherent property of the data.
