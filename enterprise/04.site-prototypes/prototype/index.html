The Solution

Private data. AI-native. Real-time.

Data Privacy

Private by Default

Your business data stays on your infrastructure — always. AI models run locally on your hardware for sensitive workflows. Commercial APIs are available for general-purpose tasks, with architectural separation ensuring confidential data never reaches external services. No data is used for model training. No vendor ever sees your content.

AI Integration

AI-Native from the Data Layer Up

Your existing systems connect to a structured data layer through standards-based integrations. AI agents interact through typed interfaces: multi-agent workflows coordinate across functions, each agent sandboxed to its own scope and permissions. Specification-driven AI, not open-ended prompts. Every autonomous operation can require human approval.

Architecture

Real-Time and Scalable

Built on a reactive architecture: event sourcing, CQRS, event-driven processing. Business data synchronizes across devices, applications, and users in real-time. The write layer scales horizontally — each business object is an independent consistency boundary. Platform-grade performance without centralized infrastructure.

Integration

Connect everything. Expose nothing.

Parallel adoption, not rip-and-replace

Powerhouse doesn't replace your existing systems — it runs alongside them. Switchboard connects your ERP, HRIS, document management, email, and financial systems to a structured data layer where AI can operate. Your existing workflows continue unchanged.

Every integration is a data boundary

Each connection defines a scope: what data enters, what format it takes, and who — human or agent — can access it. An HR agent sees personnel records but not financial forecasts. A procurement agent sees vendor negotiations but not employee data. Isolation is defined at the integration level, not layered on as policy.

Tiered AI Model Access

Local Models
Most sensitive data — privileged legal communications, raw personnel files, board-level financials. Open-source models on your hardware. Nothing leaves your network.
Frontier Commercial Models
Less sensitive but still confidential work — operating on censored summaries, redacted extracts, or pre-approved data shapes.
General-Purpose Models
Non-confidential tasks — drafting, formatting, research, translation.
Your DPO defines the tiers. The architecture enforces them.

Privacy & Compliance

Built into the architecture.
Not bolted on.

The section the CTO sends to the DPO, legal counsel, and compliance team.

Data Residency

All business data is stored on infrastructure you control. Self-host on your own servers, or deploy on Vetra Cloud with contractual data residency guarantees. No data replication to third-party systems unless you explicitly configure it.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every operation — human or AI — is cryptographically signed, timestamped, and appended to an immutable history. The audit trail is not a log; it is the data structure. SOX, GDPR, HIPAA: compliance is structural.

Ready for What's Coming

The EU AI Act and emerging sector-specific AI regulations will require transparency into AI decision-making, data provenance tracking, and algorithmic accountability. Powerhouse's architecture — immutable operation history, cryptographic attribution of every AI action, specification-driven agent behavior — is structurally ready for these requirements. Organizations adopting this infrastructure now won't need to retrofit compliance when regulation arrives.

Data Portability

Your data is JSON. Your schemas are typed definitions you own. Your application code is TypeScript. If you leave, everything comes with you. The entire stack is open source.
