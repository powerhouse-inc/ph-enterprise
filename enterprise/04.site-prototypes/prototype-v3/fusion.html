What Fusion Does

Your intranet, rebuilt on live data.

Next.js-based internal platform. Reads from Switchboard and presents business data, workflows, dashboards, and AI outputs to every department.

What It Replaces

SharePoint portals nobody updates. Scattered dashboards built by three teams. Stale wikis. The gap between where data lives and where people work.

What It Provides

One workspace across departments with customized views. AI insights alongside live data. Whitelabel — your IT team owns and extends it.

Architecture

Built on your data layer. Standards-based.

Switchboard-Backed

Reads and writes through Switchboard's GraphQL API. Authorization, scoping, and audit enforcement happen at the Switchboard layer — inherited automatically.

Customizable Modules

Finance dashboards, HR self-service, procurement tracking, legal summaries. Each module queries only authorized data.

Verification and Audit

Inspect the full history of any data — who changed it, when, and how. AI-generated content labeled and traceable.

Extensible

Standard Next.js development. React and TypeScript — skills your team already has. No proprietary framework.

Use Cases

Built for real internal applications.

Executive Dashboards

Real-time financials, headcount, project status, compliance metrics. AI summaries and anomaly flags alongside live data.

Department Portals

HR, procurement, legal, finance — each department sees exactly what's relevant, scoped by Switchboard authorization.

Audit & Compliance Views

Full operation history for any business object. Exportable audit trails for regulatory review. Every state change attributed to a verified identity.

Stakeholder Reporting

External-facing views too — investor dashboards, partner portals, client pages — with Switchboard data scoping.

Technical Details
FrameworkNext.js (React, TypeScript)
Data sourceSwitchboard GraphQL API — all data access through the integration hub
AuthorizationInherited from Switchboard — per-user, per-role, per-department scoping
DeploymentSelf-hosted or Vetra Cloud
CustomizationStandard React/Next.js development; no proprietary component framework
