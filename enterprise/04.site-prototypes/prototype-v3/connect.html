What Connect Does

Where your teams do the work.

Structured Business Data

Every business object follows a typed schema. Data is valid by construction, not by manual validation after the fact.

Drive Apps & Workflow Views

Workflow-specific interfaces overlay the file system — opinionated views built for how your team actually works.

Real-Time Collaboration

Multiple team members on the same objects simultaneously. Changes sync through operation histories — attributed, versioned, auto-mergeable.

Human Oversight of AI

AI agents act on the same data your team sees. Every agent-generated change is visible, reviewable, and requires approval before taking effect.

Offline-Capable

Local-first architecture. Operations queue locally and sync when connectivity returns.

Cryptographic Verification

Every operation signed by its author — human or agent. Complete history verifiable offline. Tamper-proof audit is a property of the data, not a feature.
