What Fusion Does

Your intranet, rebuilt on live data.

Fusion is a Next.js-based internal platform framework. It reads from your Switchboard data layer and presents business data, workflows, dashboards, and AI outputs to every department.

What It Replaces

SharePoint-style portals that nobody updates. Scattered departmental dashboards built by three different teams. Internal wikis that went stale last quarter. The gap between where your data lives and where your people work.

What It Provides

One workspace across departments, each with its own customized view. AI-generated insights surface alongside live operational data. Fully whitelabel — your IT team owns it, extends it, and deploys it on your infrastructure.

Architecture

Built on your data layer. Standards-based.

Switchboard-Backed

Fusion reads and writes through Switchboard's GraphQL API. Authorization, data scoping, and audit enforcement all happen at the Switchboard layer — so Fusion inherits every privacy and compliance guarantee automatically.

Customizable Modules

Build what each department needs: finance dashboards, HR self-service portals, procurement tracking, legal matter summaries. Each module queries only the business data it's authorized to access.

Verification and Audit

Users can inspect the full history of any data they see — who changed it, when, and how. AI-generated content is clearly labeled and traceable to the agent that produced it.

Extensible

Standard Next.js development. Your frontend team builds custom pages, widgets, and integrations with React and TypeScript — skills they already have. No proprietary component framework to learn.

Use Cases

Built for real internal applications.

Executive Dashboards

Real-time organizational overview — financials, headcount, project status, compliance metrics. AI-generated summaries and anomaly flags surface alongside the live data, not in a separate tool.

Department Portals

HR self-service, procurement tracking, legal matter status, finance reporting. Each department sees exactly what's relevant to them, scoped by Switchboard authorization rules.

Audit & Compliance Views

Full operation history for any business object. Exportable audit trails for regulatory review. Every state change attributed to a verified identity.

Stakeholder Reporting

When needed, Fusion serves external-facing views too — investor dashboards, partner portals, client status pages — with appropriate data scoping at the Switchboard layer.
