The Solution

Data Privacy

Private by Default

Your business data never leaves your infrastructure. Sensitive workflows run on local AI models deployed on your hardware. Commercial APIs handle only general-purpose tasks — with architectural separation that ensures confidential data never reaches external services. No vendor ever sees your content. No data is used for model training.

AI Integration

AI-Native from the Data Layer Up

Forget bolting AI onto legacy systems. Powerhouse gives your existing data a structured layer with typed interfaces that AI agents can operate on directly. Multiple specialized agents coordinate across business functions — each sandboxed to its own data scope, permissions, and model tier. Agents follow structured playbooks, not open-ended prompts. Every autonomous action can require human approval.

Architecture

Real-Time, Scalable, Auditable

Built on proven patterns — event sourcing, CQRS, reactive processing — composed into a single document primitive. Business data synchronizes across devices, applications, and users in real-time. Every business object is an independent consistency boundary, so the write layer scales horizontally. No centralized bottleneck.

Integration

Connect everything. Expose nothing.

Runs alongside what you have

Powerhouse doesn't replace your ERP, HRIS, or financial systems. Switchboard connects them to a structured data layer where AI can operate — without touching your existing workflows. You adopt incrementally, starting with the business processes that benefit most.

Every connection is a data boundary

Each integration defines exactly what data enters, what shape it takes, and who can access it. Your HR agent sees personnel records but not financial forecasts. Your procurement agent sees vendor data but not employee records. This isolation is architectural, not a policy layer you hope people follow.

Privacy & Compliance

Built into the architecture.
Not bolted on.

Forward this section to your DPO, legal counsel, and compliance team.

Data Residency

Every byte of business data is stored on infrastructure you control. Self-host on your own servers or deploy on Vetra Cloud with contractual residency guarantees. Nothing replicates to third-party systems unless you explicitly configure it.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every operation — human or AI — is cryptographically signed, timestamped, and appended to an immutable history. This isn't a log bolted onto the side. The audit trail is the data structure. SOX, GDPR, HIPAA compliance is structural, not procedural.

Ready for What's Coming

The EU AI Act and sector-specific regulations are coming. They'll require transparency into AI decision-making, data provenance tracking, and algorithmic accountability. Powerhouse already meets these requirements architecturally: immutable operation history, cryptographic attribution of every AI action, specification-driven agent behavior. Organizations on this infrastructure won't need to retrofit compliance when regulation arrives — they'll already be there.

Data Portability

Your data is JSON. Your schemas are TypeScript definitions you own. Your code is yours. If you leave, everything comes with you — no proprietary format, no extraction negotiation. The entire stack is open source.
