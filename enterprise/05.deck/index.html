Not all data should reach the same model.

Local models — Most sensitive

Privileged legal communications, raw personnel files, board-level financials, trade secrets. Open-source models on your hardware. Nothing leaves your network.

Frontier commercial — Confidential, censored

Redacted extracts, sanitized summaries, pre-approved data shapes. The integration layer controls what reaches the external model.

General-purpose — Non-confidential

Drafting, formatting, research, translation. Standard commercial APIs for tasks with no sensitivity constraint.

Your DPO defines the tiers. The architecture enforces them.
