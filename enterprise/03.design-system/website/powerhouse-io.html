Local First

Own your data. Work offline. Sync when you're ready. Local-first means your documents, tools, and workflows live on your device first and the network second. No landlord. No lock-in.

Decentralized Technologies

Real-time collaboration without a central server requires peer-to-peer protocols and decentralized networks. These systems let users and devices sync directly, turning documents into live communication channels and applications into collaborative tools.
